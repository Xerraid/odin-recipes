How to Make Perfect Honey Coated- Walnut Stuffed Dates

**Instructions**
    1.**Prepare the dates:** Slice each Medjool date lengthwise on one side and remove the pits. Be careful not to cut all the way through.
    2. **Make the filling:** In a small bowl, mix the chopped walnuts, honey, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Add the orange zest or orange blossom water if using for a citrusy aroma.
    3. **Stuff the dates:** Using a small spoon, stuff each date with the walnut mixture. Press gently to close the dates around the filling.
    4. **Finish with a drizzle:** Arrange the stuffed dates on a serving platter and drizzle lightly with extra honey.
    5. **Garnish (optional):** Sprinkle with crushed pistachios or shredded coconut for added texture and visual appeal.
    6. **Serve:** These stuffed dates can be served immediately or chilled for a slightly firmer texture. Theyâ€™re perfect as a snack, dessert, or part of a mezze platter.
